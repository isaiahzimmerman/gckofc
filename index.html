Who are the Knights?
The Knights of Columbus is a Catholic fraternal organization founded in 1882 by Blessed Michael J. McGivney. It is a global organization limited to practicing Catholic men, dedicated to charitable, religious, and social welfare works, providing mutual aid, insurance products, and assistance to members and their families.
Charity
The Knights of Columbus fundraises for and donates to many local, national and international charities.
